Welcome to Buypartisan! (FIRST DRAFT TUTORIAL - TYLER WELSH)
The goal of the game is to accumulate the most victory points by the end of Buypartisan.
You obtain victory points by winning elections, (AND OTHER THINGS?).
You win elections by gathering the most voters (AND OTHER THINGS?).
Players gain voters by "capturing" them in their sphere of influence, indicated by the sphere of their color.
Voters also give the player money which can be spent on actions to change the course of their campeign.
